The Holy Goose Bible

1:SWAN

One day, an embden goose, our lord and savior, appeared in outer space. He then created earth, modeled it by hand, and created two beings known as Cow and Bull. They were allowed to eat any fruit in the world except for the Tree of the Goose. One day, an evil being, known as the Shark of Death, rises from the water and convinces Cow to eat the forbidden tree of the Goose. He said "Thy shalt eat the forbidden tree to save me." Cow was manipulated by the Shark and eventually ate the fruit and convinced Bull to eat it too. The Goose found out and took away their skin and let them burn in Hell. The Holy Goose, our lord and savior, then replaced the two with St. Duck and St. Swan. The Shark was evaporated and placed inside the middle of Earth's core.

2:SWAN

The Holy Goose, also known as the Great Leader of All, made himself and the Saints immortal. St. Duck created more ducks by creating Saintess Duck, and Saint Swan did the same. They got married and had children which eventually spread to up to 100 million ducks and swans. All descendants of The Great Leader of All, the Two Saints, or everyone who believes in Gooseism is known as a gooseling.
